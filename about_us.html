About Us

This website has an educational purpose and is focusing on the touchscreen technology. The Lessons chapter of this website is divided in 3 articles (lessons).

  1. First lesson will introduce you to the general idea of the touchscreen technology and where this technology can be found.

  2. The second lesson is dedicated to the history of touchscreen technology, in which we tried to pin-point few dates and names related with the development of these technology.

  3. In the third lesson you will find some more in depth information about how the touchscreen technology is working. This lesson is accompanied with a detailed animated video which will help you having a better understanding of this technology.

We hope you will enjoy and get the most from our lessons!
Good luck!
